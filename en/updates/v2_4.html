Word Book

Some IDEs (such as WebStorm) may require you to download the support library to use the Word Book. Simply click "Download" in the Word Book board and wait for the download to complete.

Word of the Day

The Word of the Day displays random words daily from the Word Book, one by one. You can also enable it by using the One Word a Day action (no default shortcut).

You can also display these words in the IDE at startup by checking the "Show words on startup" option on the Settings page. In addition, Word of the Day hides word definitions by default. To disable hiding definitions, check the "Show word explanation" option on the Settings page.
